    About Me

    Graduating from Clemson University with a degree in Graphic Communications, I started out my career as a freelance graphic designer. One day a client asked me if I knew how to create websites. Not wanting to turn away business so early in my career, I replied "Yes".

    Like many other developers, my journey began with WordPress. After about a year manipulating WP themes, I realized I was missing critical skills needed to reach the next level, in particular JavaScript.

    After consulting with family and friends, I discovered The Iron Yard, an intensive 3-month web development bootcamp. This is where I learned the fundamentals of JavaScript, gained additional knowledge of CSS and HTML and learned the basics of frameworks like Backbone, Ember & Angular.

    Outside of coding, I enjoy playing league tennis, pickup basketball, following Clemson sports, FanDuel, beating my friends in fantasy football and enjoying an occasional cold IPA.
